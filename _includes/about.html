About ADASTACK

We have run a successful top performing pool since December 2019,
through the ITN and currently Mainnet.

We care very much about Cardano, we are a member of
Cardano Friends and Family,
Cardano Social Council,
Stake Pool Operator Collective Representation Assembly and
Inter Stake Pool Peering Agreement along with
being a patreon of PoolTool and ADApools.

The pool passes 98% of block rewards to delegators and claims 2%
with the minimum pool cost set.
